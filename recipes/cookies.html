almond flour chocolate chip cookies

description

ingredients

  • 2 3/4 c almond flour
  • 1/2 c brown sugar
  • 1/2 c granulated sugar
  • 1/2 c butter, room temperature
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 12 ounces chocolate chips
  • flaky sea salt

steps

  1. heat oven to 350 degrees
  2. line two baking sheets with parchment paper
  3. whisk together almond flour, salt and baking soda
  4. in a mixer bowl, cream the butter and both sugars on medium speed until very light, 3-4 minutes
  5. add the egg and mix on medium speed to combine. scrape the bowl, add vanilla and mix to combine
  6. add the dry ingredients and mix on low 10 seconds. scrape the bowl and make sure it is all combined
  7. add the chocolate chips and mix gently
  8. chill dough in the fridge for 30 minutes
  9. scoop dough into golf ball sized cookies, pressing down gently until shaped like a cookie 1/2 inch thick
  10. sprinkle with flaky sea salt, use your heart to measure
  11. bake 18-22 min, switching racks & rotating halfway through until edges are golden brown and cookies are just barely set in the center. cool for atleast 10 minutes
